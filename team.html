ABOUT US

Purefy is a national online lender offering student loan refinancing. We started in 2014 as a product launch from a community bank and today we are a growing company with offices in Washington, DC and Richmond VA. Our mission is to help college graduates and parents get out of student debt faster. We offer low interest rates and 5, 8, 10, 12, 15 and 20-year5, 8, 12 and 15-year terms to student loan borrowers who have earned their degree and launched their careers.
